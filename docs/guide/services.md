# Professional Services

The NIS2 Compliance Platform is developed and maintained by **Fabrizio Salmi**, independent cybersecurity and compliance consultant specializing in the NIS2 Directive (EU 2022/2555) and its Italian transposition (D.Lgs 138/2024).

## Available Services

### Private NIS2 Compliance Scan

Full infrastructure scanning with white-label executive reports. Includes:
- Complete Art. 21 compliance assessment (all 10 subsections)
- Certificate chain analysis with remediation priorities
- DNS security audit (SPF, DMARC, DKIM, DNSSEC)
- Port exposure analysis with risk classification
- Deliverable: Executive PDF report + technical findings spreadsheet

### Certificate Lifecycle Management

End-to-end TLS/SSL certificate remediation powered by [CertMate](https://github.com/fabriziosalmi/certmate) and [CertMate-NG](https://github.com/fabriziosalmi/certmate-ng):
- Certificate inventory and expiry tracking
- Chain validation and intermediate certificate issues
- Automated renewal pipeline setup (certbot, acme.sh)
- OCSP and CT log monitoring
- Key strength upgrades (RSA to ECDSA migration)

### Custom Readiness Assessment

Gap analysis tailored to your sector and organizational structure:
- Mapping of existing controls to NIS2 Art. 21 requirements
- Risk assessment with CVSS-based severity scoring
- Prioritized remediation roadmap with effort and cost estimates
- Board-ready compliance posture presentation

### Incident Response Consulting

Support for CSIRT notification requirements under Art. 23:
- Incident taxonomy classification
- Timeline reconstruction and evidence preservation
- Notification drafting (early warning, incident notification, final report)
- Post-incident review and lessons learned

### Ongoing Compliance Monitoring

Continuous compliance assurance:
- Scheduled weekly/monthly scans with trend analysis
- Quarterly compliance reports with score progression
- Certificate expiry alerts (30/15/7-day warnings)
- New vulnerability detection and prioritization

### Platform Customization

Tailored deployments for specific organizational needs:
- Private on-premise or cloud deployment
- Sector-specific scanner modules (healthcare, finance, energy)
- Custom report templates with corporate branding
- Integration with existing SIEM/SOAR/ticketing systems
- MCP server configuration for AI-assisted compliance workflows

### Staff Training

NIS2 awareness and technical training:
- Board-level NIS2 overview (obligations, penalties, timeline)
- Technical security training for development and operations teams
- Hands-on platform training for internal compliance officers
